## Variable Guide (Instantly.ai)

Use these standard Instantly.ai variables in your sequences:

- `{{first_name}}` - Prospect's first name
- `{{company_name}}` - Company name
- `{{company_website}}` - Company website URL
- `{{industry}}` - Industry/vertical

**Custom Variables to Add:**
- `CALENDLY_LINK` - Replace with your actual Calendly scheduling link
- Personalization tokens based on your research (e.g., specific pain points)

## Sequence 1: Bad Website Outreach

**Target Audience:** Businesses with outdated, slow, or non-mobile-optimized websites
**Best For:** Local businesses, SMBs, service providers, restaurants, retail
**Timing:** 3 emails over 8 days
**Expected Response Rate:** 3-8%

### Email 1 (Day 1)
**Subject:** Quick question about {{company_name}}'s website

**Body:**
```

I was looking at {{company_website}} and noticed [it's not mobile-optimized / loads slowly / has an outdated design].

We just rebuilt a similar site for a [industry] business and they saw a 40% increase in leads within the first month.

We use AI-accelerated development so we deliver in days, not months — and at a fraction of what traditional agencies charge.

Worth a 15-min chat?

[CALENDLY_LINK]

— Chetan, The Chadha Group
```

**Notes:**
- Personalize the bracketed section with actual observation
- Replace [industry] with prospect's industry
- Keep tone helpful, not salesy

## Best Practices for Instantly.ai

### Timing Guidelines
- **Email 1:** Day 0 (immediate)
- **Email 2:** Day 3-4
- **Email 3:** Day 7-8
- **DO NOT** send more than 3 emails in first sequence
- Space emails 3-4 days apart minimum

### Subject Line Tips
- Keep under 50 characters
- Use company name when possible
- Avoid spam triggers (FREE, !!!, URGENT)
- Test A/B variants

### Writing Best Practices
- ✅ Short paragraphs (1-2 lines max)
- ✅ Specific value propositions
- ✅ Single call-to-action
- ✅ Conversational tone
- ❌ No walls of text
- ❌ No jargon or buzzwords
- ❌ No multiple asks

### Personalization Checklist
Before sending, customize:
- [ ] Industry-specific pain point
- [ ] Relevant case study
- [ ] Actual website observation
- [ ] Appropriate pricing tier
- [ ] Correct Calendly link

## A/B Testing Recommendations

Test these variants:

**Subject Lines:**
- Question vs. statement
- Company name vs. no company name
- Value prop vs. curiosity

**Body Copy:**
- Short (3 lines) vs. medium (5-6 lines)
- Question-led vs. statement-led
- Price mentioned vs. no price

**CTA:**
- Calendar link vs. reply request
- Specific time vs. open availability

## Response Handling

### Positive Response
```
Great! I'll send over a calendar link — pick any time that works for you.

In the meantime, what's the biggest challenge you're facing with [website/automation]?

Looking forward to chatting!
```

### Neutral/Question Response
```
Happy to clarify!

[Answer their question briefly]

Would a quick 15-min call be easier to discuss? Here's my calendar: [CALENDLY_LINK]
```

### "Not Interested" Response
```
No problem at all — I appreciate you letting me know!

If anything changes down the road, feel free to reach out. Best of luck with {{company_name}}!
```

### "Send Me Info" Response
```
Absolutely! Here's a quick overview:

[2-3 bullet points about service]
[Link to case study or portfolio]

Would you like to jump on a quick call to discuss your specific needs? [CALENDLY_LINK]
```

## Campaign Setup in Instantly.ai

### Recommended Settings
- **Daily send limit:** 50-100 emails/day (warm up gradually)
- **Time zone:** Prospect's local time (8am-5pm)
- **Open tracking:** Enabled
- **Click tracking:** Enabled
- **Auto-stop on reply:** Enabled
- **Unsubscribe link:** Include in footer

## Metrics to Track

**Key Performance Indicators:**
- Open rate: Target 40-60%
- Reply rate: Target 3-8%
- Positive reply rate: Target 1-3%
- Meeting booked rate: Target 0.5-2%

**Red Flags (Stop and Adjust):**
- Open rate < 30%
- Reply rate < 1%
- High spam complaints
- Domain reputation drop

---

## Legal Compliance

✅ **CAN-SPAM Compliance:**
- Include physical address in footer
- Add unsubscribe link
- Honor opt-outs within 10 days
- Use accurate "From" name and subject

✅ **GDPR (if targeting EU):**
- Ensure legitimate interest basis
- Allow data deletion requests
- Don't use purchased lists
